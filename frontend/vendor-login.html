Scoped Vendor Sandbox

Sign in to your vendor sandbox.

This portal is for external pentesters and VAPT auditors invited by a URIP customer. You’ll only see what your client’s admin has explicitly scoped to your engagement.

  • Submit findings via a structured form — CVE ID, CVSS, PoC artifact, affected asset.
  • See your own submissions and re-test requests.
  • Watch each finding move from submitted → assigned → remediated.
  • You will not see internal network data, other vendors’ findings, or the customer’s native connector outputs.
  • Your access auto-expires at the end of the engagement window.
